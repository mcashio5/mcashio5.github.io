About Dragonfly Homes Page Design (about.html)

  • Name of the page: About Dragonfly Homes
  • Purpose of the page: Provide background on Dragonfly Homes, highlight Brittney’s experience, and build trust with potential buyers.
  • Audience/Users of page: New visitors who want to know more about the business and its owner.
  • Content of the page:
    • Short business history and mission statement.
    • Photos of past staging projects.
    • Bullet list of services (staging, consultations, styling).
  • Is this a page where you are asking users to enter data? No.
  • Will those data fields need any validations? Not applicable.
  • Buttons, hyperlinks, drop downs:
    • Links back to Home and Shop pages.
    • Possibly a “Book a staging consultation” link that jumps to the Contact page form.
  • Actions that will happen on the page:
    • Smooth scrolling or navigation to the Contact form when a “Book now” button is clicked.
  • Special notes: This page could include a jQuery UI accordion for “Services” that expands and collapses details.
