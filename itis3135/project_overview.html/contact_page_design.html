How to Buy / Contact Page Design (contact.html)

  • Name of the page: How to Buy / Contact
  • Purpose of the page: Explain how the purchasing process works and provide a form for users to ask about specific items or schedule pickup.
  • Audience/Users of page: Serious buyers ready to contact Dragonfly Homes.
  • Content of the page:
    • Step-by-step explanation of how to inquire about a piece, confirm availability, and arrange payment and pickup.
    • Contact form fields: Name, Email, Phone (optional), Item of Interest, Message.
    • FAQ section about condition, holds, and pickup times.
  • Is this a page where you are asking users to enter data? Yes, users will submit a contact form.
  • Will those data fields need any validations?
    • Required fields: Name, Email, and Message.
    • Email must match a basic email pattern.
    • Optional phone field can be validated for numeric characters and length.
  • Buttons, hyperlinks, drop downs:
    • Submit button for the contact form.
    • Clear/Reset button (optional).
    • Links back to Shop Inventory and Home pages.
  • Actions that will happen on the page:
    • JavaScript validates the form on submit and shows friendly error messages if any required fields are missing or invalid.
    • If the form passes validation, the page will show a confirmation message and could send the data to an email endpoint or a placeholder “success” page (depending on what is allowed in the course).
  • Special notes: The FAQ section below the form may use a jQuery UI accordion for collapsible questions and answers.
