Home Page Design (index.html)

  • Name of the page: Home
  • Purpose of the page: Introduce Dragonfly Homes and explain the purpose of the furniture sale, while directing visitors quickly to the Shop and Gallery.
  • Audience/Users of page: All visitors, including homeowners, renters, realtors, and staging clients.
  • Content of the page:
    • Short description of Dragonfly Homes and the surplus furniture sale.
    • Hero image from a staged home.
    • Three featured category cards (Living Room, Bedroom, Dining).
    • A small “How it works” section that describes browsing, inquiring, and pickup.
  • Is this a page where you are asking users to enter data? No.
  • Will those data fields need any validations? Not applicable.
  • Buttons, hyperlinks, drop downs:
    • Navigation bar links to all pages.
    • “Shop Now” button linking to Shop Inventory.
    • “View Room Ideas” button linking to Styled Rooms Gallery.
  • Actions that will happen on the page:
    • Clicking call-to-action buttons navigates to Shop or Gallery pages.
    • Featured cards link to filtered views of the Shop page (for example, Living Room items only).
  • Special notes: This page sets the tone for the site, so imagery and branding for Dragonfly Homes are especially important.
