About Dragonfly Homes

Dragonfly Homes is a residential staging company in the Charlotte area, led by owner and designer Brittney Wall. The team stages occupied and vacant homes using neutral, welcoming furniture and decor that help buyers imagine living in the space.

Dragonfly Home Market grew out of a simple problem: beautiful pieces that no longer fit active staging projects were piling up in storage. Rather than letting them sit unused, this catalog offers those items for sale to local homeowners, renters, and agents.

Services

  • Vacant home staging
  • Occupied home refreshes
  • Room-by-room styling and consultations
