How to Buy

Steps to purchase

  1. Browse the Shop Inventory and note the item names that interest you.
  2. Fill out the contact form below with your preferred items and any questions.
  3. Dragonfly Homes will confirm availability, answer questions, and send pickup details.
  4. Arrange pickup and payment, then take your staged piece home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the items new or used?
Most items have been lightly used in staged homes and may show minor wear. Condition details are listed on each product card in the Shop.
Do you offer delivery?
Delivery is not included by default. In some cases, local delivery may be arranged for an additional fee. Include this question in your message if needed.
Can you hold an item for me?
Items are available on a first come, first served basis. Short holds may be possible once pickup has been scheduled.
