About Mighty Chicken

Mighty Chicken began as a design exercise and became a tiny laboratory for joyful, chicken-themed products. Each release is intentionally small, thoughtfully packaged, and easy to gift.

We keep categories limited—spice kits, enamel pins, desktop mascots—so you’re never overwhelmed. Everything is built with accessibility and sustainability in mind: clear labels, high-contrast design, recyclable materials, and simple instructions.

Why “Mighty Chicken”? It matches the mascot rule (M + C), it’s memorable, and it gives us endless themes without needing a huge catalog.
